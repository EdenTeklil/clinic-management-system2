WE TAKE CARE OF YOUR HEALTHY LIFE

Our Obstetrics and Gynecology Department at Emirates Hospital is a well reputed department in the region with a highly committed and skilled clinical team. Our broad spectrum of services includes adolescent health programme, preconception counselling, reproductive medicine, pre-natal classes, preventive health programme, high-risk pregnancy care, contraceptive medicine, IVF programme, menopausal care, assisted conception and infertility solutions. We also provide, complete outpatient and in-patient gynaecological care, using the latest Minimally Invasive modalities like Laparoscopic, Hysteroscopic and Colposcopic procedures for various gynaecological disorders like Fibroids, Endometriosis, abnormal Uterine Bleeding. Vaginal surgeries and conventional abdominal surgeries are also done with great expertise.
